    About us

    Women Techmakers Amsterdam is part of Google's Women Techmakers global program and part of the GDG Netherlands community. We provide visibility, community, and resources for women in technology. In the Netherlands, through regular events, workshops, support groups and sharing of resources, we support the local female tech community. The community includes women in tech as well as those who aspire to get into the industry. Read more about our activities under Events and join us to get involved!

    =======

    ManizhaManizha is the Lead for WTM Amsterdam. She is a full stack developer as well as a marketing professional, who combines the two disciplines to cook up new stuff. She is originally from Tajikistan and has at different times called Czechia, Germany, Sweden and the middle of nowhere in Iceland her home before settling in the Netherlands. Manizha's programming journey started with a coding bootcamp and she hasn't looked back since. She now strives to support women to build their careers in tech and can’t wait to grow the WTM community in Amsterdam.
